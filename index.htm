Black Soldier Fly: A high-quality feed for poultry and fish

  • Excellent source of high quality protein and healthy fats containing essential amino acids and unsaturated fatty acids that are crucial for poultry health.

    Rich in vitamins such as B vitamins (thiamine, riboflavin, niacin, biotin and vitamin B12) and minerals such as calcium, phosphorus, iron and zinc, which are essential for growth and reproduction.

    Highly digestible for poultry, ensuring efficient nutrient absorption. As a complete and balanced food source, black soldier fly larvae contribute to the overall health and performance of poultry.
